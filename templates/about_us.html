About ShopMax

Revolutionizing campus commerce at Uganda Christian University

Our Story

Founded in 2024, ShopMax emerged from a simple idea: to create a trusted marketplace specifically for the UCU community. We noticed that students and staff needed a safe, reliable platform to buy and sell goods within the campus ecosystem.

What started as a student project has grown into UCU's premier online marketplace, connecting buyers and sellers in a secure, user-friendly environment.

Safe & Secure

Verified users and secure transactions protect our community

Fast Delivery

Campus-wide delivery within 24 hours

Community First

Built for UCU by UCU community members

Our Mission

To empower the UCU community by providing a seamless platform for commerce, fostering entrepreneurship, and building trust among students, staff, and local businesses.

Our Vision

To become the leading campus marketplace in East Africa, revolutionizing how students and educational communities engage in commerce.
