Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I'm not home when my delivery arrives? +

If you're not home, our delivery partner will attempt to leave your package in a safe place. If this isn't possible, they will leave a note with instructions on how to reschedule delivery or pick up your package from a local facility.

Can I change my delivery address after placing an order? +

Yes, you can change your delivery address if your order hasn't been shipped yet. Please contact our customer service team as soon as possible with your order number and the updated address.

Do you deliver on weekends and holidays? +

We offer Saturday delivery as an additional service for an extra fee. We do not deliver on Sundays or major holidays. Delivery times may be extended during holiday periods.

What should I do if my package is damaged? +

If your package arrives damaged, please contact our customer service team within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange for a replacement or refund as appropriate.
