About This Student Project
ShopMax UCU - Final Year Project

This platform was developed by final year Computer Science students at Uganda Christian University as part of our capstone project.

Project Goal: To create a safe, efficient marketplace exclusively for the UCU community where students and staff can buy, sell, and trade items within our campus ecosystem.

Supervised by: Department of Computing & Technology, UCU
