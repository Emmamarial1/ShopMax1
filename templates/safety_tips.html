Safe Transactions

Buying Safety Guide

Protect yourself when purchasing on ShopMax

Research Sellers

Check seller ratings, reviews, and verification status before purchasing. Look for sellers with established history.

Examine Product Photos

Look for clear, multiple photos from different angles. Be cautious of stock images or blurry photos.

Ask Questions

Communicate with sellers through our secure messaging system. Ask about product condition, authenticity, and details.

Get Documentation

Request receipts, warranty information, or proof of purchase for high-value items.

DO'S FOR BUYERS
  • Verify seller ratings and reviews
  • Use secure payment methods
  • Meet in safe, public locations
  • Inspect items before payment
  • Keep communication on platform
DON'TS FOR BUYERS
  • Don't pay outside the platform
  • Don't share personal information
  • Don't meet in private locations
  • Don't rush the transaction
  • Don't ignore red flags

Selling Safety Guide

Protect your business and yourself when selling

Verify Buyers

Check buyer profiles and ratings. Be cautious of new accounts with no history.

Secure Payments

Only accept payments through ShopMax. Avoid cash transactions for high-value items.

Document Everything

Take photos/videos of items before handing over. Keep records of all transactions.

Safe Delivery

Use our verified delivery partners. Get delivery confirmation for all shipments.

DO'S FOR SELLERS
  • Verify buyer profiles
  • Use secure payment methods
  • Meet in safe locations
  • Keep transaction records
  • Report suspicious activity
DON'TS FOR SELLERS
  • Don't share bank details
  • Don't meet buyers alone
  • Don't accept offline payments
  • Don't ship before payment
  • Don't ignore buyer reviews

Online Safety & Security

Protect your digital presence and personal information

Red Flags to Watch For
  • Prices that seem too good to be true
  • Sellers requesting payment outside ShopMax
  • Pressure to complete transaction quickly
  • Vague or incomplete product descriptions
  • Requests for personal information
  • New accounts with no verification
Account Security Tips
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Never share login credentials
  • Log out from shared devices
  • Monitor account activity regularly
  • Update contact information
Beware of Phishing Attempts
How to Spot Fake ShopMax Communications:
  • Check sender's email address carefully
  • Look for poor grammar and spelling
  • Hover over links before clicking
  • Never enter password from email links
  • Official emails will address you by name
